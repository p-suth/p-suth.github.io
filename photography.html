Past Projects

I have photographed well over a hundred bands ranging from festivals to basements. My concert photography originates in my love for experimental music communities. Going with a camera is mostly a way to add an extra dimension to the gig, and to give something back to to the bands. Besides that I love the challenge of having to work with bad light and fast-moving subjects in cramped conditions - I have to work with what I'm given, keeping my eye out for interesting dynamics, light and structural patterns, or just ways to get a clear shot of the drummer. I have a firm ethic of, as much as possible, not disrupting the experiences of fellow attendees, by limiting my own presence and avoiding flash photography.

My concert photographs have been used in The Independent (UK), SPREAD, Fecking Bahamas, and other websites, and as cover and promotional photographs for festivals and bands.

I also do documentary and art photography, and am looking to grow this part of my work.
